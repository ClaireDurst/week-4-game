Crystal Collector

Instructions


    You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Match This Number"

    There are four crystals below.

    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow

    By clicking on a crystal, you will add a random amount of points to your total score.

    You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

    If your score goes above the random number, you lose the game.

    The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

    Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
